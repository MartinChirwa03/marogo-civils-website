Our Journey of Excellence

From humble beginnings to industry leadership, discover the story behind MAROGO Civils Limited

2018 - Our Foundation

MAROGO Civils Limited was born from a vision to transform Malawi's construction landscape. Officially registered under the Companies Act No. 15 of 2013, we began our journey with unwavering commitment to excellence and integrity.

Growth & Recognition

Through dedication and superior project delivery, we achieved rapid growth and earned our place in the 500 million Kwacha category by the National Construction Industry Council (NCIC) - a testament to our expanding capabilities and trusted reputation.

Industry Leadership

Today, we stand as a significant player in Malawi's construction industry, not just building structures, but building relationships and contributing to national development with every project we undertake.

Our Mission Today

We continue to set new benchmarks in quality, efficiency, and sustainability within Malawi's construction landscape. Our classification in the 500 million Kwacha NCIC category reflects our unwavering commitment to excellence and our growing capacity to handle complex projects.
